Why it exists

Trading research gets fragmented fast.

Analysts often jump between market data, indicators, manual spreadsheets, news, social context, and portfolio-level performance views. QuantAlgo consolidates that exploratory workflow into a single dashboard built for fast iteration and clear decision support.

Core system

Everything needed to move from ticker idea to strategy readout.

01

Market data

Fetch and inspect configurable assets using Yahoo Finance data across common research intervals.

02

Technical strategies

Explore moving average crossovers, RSI mean reversion, and volatility regimes with adjustable inputs.

03

Backtesting engine

Generate entries, exits, PnL, win rate, and drawdown metrics for strategy experiments.

04

Portfolio metrics

Review performance and risk views that make strategy behavior easier to compare and explain.

05

Sentiment analysis

Use Reddit and Google News context with VADER scoring to add market narrative signals.

06

Interactive visuals

Plotly-style dashboards expose trend, volatility, trade markers, and summary states at a glance.

Workflow

A compact research loop for rapid strategy exploration.

  1. Select asset

    Choose a ticker, interval, and date range.

  2. Configure strategy

    Tune MA windows, RSI thresholds, or volatility settings.

  3. Backtest

    Convert rules into trades and performance history.

  4. Analyze performance

    Review PnL, drawdown, win rate, and risk metrics.

  5. Compare context

    Layer in sentiment and market narrative signals.
